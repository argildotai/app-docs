There are two plans available:&#x20;

### Classic plan 39$

* 25 minutes of video per month

* 3 avatar trainings (in total)

* Magic editing

### Pro plan

* 100 minutes of video per month

* 10 avatar trainings (in total)

* Magic editing

* API access

* Pro avatars

* Priority suppport

### How to upgrade?&#x20;

Simply click the "upgrade" button at the bottom left corner.&#x20;

### How to downgrade?

At the bottom left corner click "manage plan" > "Send email" > Click the link in the email > Manage subscription
