### Account Merger

<Warning>
  If you see a merger prompt during login, **click on "continue"** to proceed.
</Warning>

![](/Captured’écran2025-01-03à00.17.22.png)
It means that you created your account with Google then via normal email for a second account but with the same address. This creates two different accounts that you need to merge.&#x20;

### Password Reset

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log out">
    Sign out of your current account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click on "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions
  </Step>
</Steps>
