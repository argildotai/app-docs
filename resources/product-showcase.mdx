### How to create an AI influencer holding your poduct from the builder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick how your avatar looks like">
    Go through all the dropdown options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your product into the product section">
    Select a picture of the product you want to add. \
    Pictures with no background and no human in it will perform best 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep playing around with other looks if needed">
    Even after you uploaded your video, you can play around and change other options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate and wait 24h-48h">
    
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to create AI actions with your influencers holding the product

<Warning>
  This only works if your AI influencer was made in the builder with the product in his hand. 
</Warning>

You can activate the fact that your avatar holds the product or not in all the AI actions. You can always activate or deactivate it specifically for each B-roll (each AI action) in the editing studio by clicking on them in the timeline.
