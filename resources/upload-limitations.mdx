---
title: "Video accepted resolutions"
---

<Info>
  Videos must match one of the following resolutions:

  * **3840 x 2160** (4K)

  * **2160 x 3840** (4K portrait)

  * **2560 x 1440** (2K)

  * **1440 x 2560** (2K portrait)

  * **1920 x 1080** (1080p)

  * **1080 x 1920** (1080p portrait)

  * **1280 x 720** (720p)

  * **720 x 1280** (720p portrait)


</Info>
