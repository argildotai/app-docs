## Adjusting Timing

* Offset helps you put the exact frame on the right word. 0.5 seconds amounts to approximately offsetting 15 frames.

* If the gesture happens too late, after what the avatar says, then click on the arrow on the right. >

* If the gesture happens too early, before what the avatar says, then click on the arrow on the left \<&#x20;
