### Changing Camera Angles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select clip">
    Click on the clip you want to modify
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose angle">
    Click "camera" in the top right and select your preferred angle
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Creating New Camera Angles

<Warning>
  Only professional avatars support multiple camera angles. User-generated content (UGC) is limited to a single view.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Record front view">
    Record yourself facing the camera directly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record side view">
    Record from a side angle (maximum 30 degrees)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Maintain consistency">
    Keep lighting, hair, and background consistent between recordings
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How It Works

<Tip>
  Our algorithm automatically switches between available camera angles to create dynamic, professional-looking videos. The transitions are timed to maintain natural flow and viewer engagement.
</Tip>

### Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I manually control camera switches?">
    Yes! Select any clip and change its camera angle using the camera option in the top right.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How many angles can I add?">
    Professional avatars support front and side angles. We recommend a maximum side angle of 30 degrees for best results.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need to record all angles at once?">
    No, but try to maintain consistent lighting, appearance, and background between recordings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
