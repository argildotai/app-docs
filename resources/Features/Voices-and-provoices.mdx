### Connect Elevenlabs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Voices">
    Select and add voices to your Elevenlabs account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="API Setup">
    Create and paste API key in Argil (voices > Elevenlabs)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Synchronize">
    Click "synchronize" after adding new voices
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Create Pro Voice

<Warning>
  Pro voices require clean audio: no pauses, hesitations, or background noise
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe">
    Get Elevenlabs creator plan
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record">
    Prepare 30 minutes of clear speech in your desired voice style
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect">
    Add API key to Argil (voices > Elevenlabs)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Link">
    Edit avatar settings to use your pro voice
  </Step>
</Steps>
