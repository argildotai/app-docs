* Your different gestures will appear chronologically in the “Body Langage” section on the right.

* To create a new clip, just press “Enter”.

* You can pick one single body langage per clip. So choose it wisely!

### How to use the offset?

Offset helps you put the exact frame on the right word. 0.5 seconds amounts to approximately offsetting 15 frames.

If the gesture happens too late, after what the avatar says, then click on the arrow on the right. >

If the gesture happens too early, before what the avatar says, then click on the arrow on the left \<
