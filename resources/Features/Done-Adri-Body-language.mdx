### What is body language ?

This is the feature that allows your avatar to move a certain way with specific gestures or emotions. You can modify the body language for each moment by selecting a clip and then clicking on the panel on the right in the studio.&#x20;

### What is offset?

Offset allows you to fine-tune the timing of your gestures. Here's how it works:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understanding offset">
    0.5 seconds equals approximately 15 frames of adjustment.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjusting late gestures">
    If the gesture happens after the speech, click the right arrow (>) to move it earlier.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjusting early gestures">
    If the gesture happens before the speech, click the left arrow (<) to move it later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
