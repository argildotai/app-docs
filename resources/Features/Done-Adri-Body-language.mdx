### Timing Adjustments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understanding Frames">
    0.5 seconds equals approximately 15 frames of adjustment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fix Late Gestures">
    Use right arrow (>) to move gestures earlier
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fix Early Gestures">
    Use left arrow (<) to move gestures later
  </Step>
</Steps>
