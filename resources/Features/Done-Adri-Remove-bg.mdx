title: "Remove background"
description: "On all the avatars available, including your own. "
---

You can pick a video or a picture as a background media.&#x20;

<Accordion title="Accepted formats (max 50 Mo)" defaultOpen={false}>
  * jpg, jpeg, png

  * mp4, mov
</Accordion>

Simply open the background panel in the studio and upload your media.&#x20;
If you want the remove background only to apply to one clip, just click on the clip, upload the image, and for the display, select "back".&#x20;
