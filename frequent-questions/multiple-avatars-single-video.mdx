---
title: "How to put multiple avatars in a single video project? "
---

<Info>
  You can have as many different avatars as you want in your videos (styles and cameras as well). Videos are made of clips, each clip can only contain one avatar.&#x20;
</Info>

### How to add multiple avatars?

Once your script is written, you'll see your video contain multiple cuts, creating different clips. &#x20;

1. Click on a clip

2. Click on "Avatar"

3. Select the one you like

4. Repeat the process with different clips

### There are 2 sentences in the same clip, how to give the first sentence to an avatar and the second to another?

Simply click in your script after the end of the first sentence. And press the return key. This will create another clip with the second sentence.&#x20;
